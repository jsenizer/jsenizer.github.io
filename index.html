Current Situation:

I'm currently searching for a postdoc (if you happen to search for a postdoc candidate in a field close to my reserach interest, I could be interested !) or a permanent position (Holy grail !).

I am interested in graph related topics, distributed computing and game theory, and more broadly about combinatorics. I mainly study, at the moment:
  • Colored graph algorithmic problems (i.e. problems whose input is a graph with colors either on the vertices or the edges, not necessarily a proper coloration)
  • Low-memory Distributed Algorithms robust to both transient and byzantine faults
  • Combinatorial games (more specifically : positional games, which are games played on hypergraphs)
